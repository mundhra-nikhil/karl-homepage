## Overview

This document outlines the **end-to-end purchasing, onboarding, and activation process** for **Karl – Insight Agent**, the enterprise AI analytics platform developed by **Kanerika Inc**. It serves as a comprehensive reference for customers, partners, and internal stakeholders to understand how organizations can evaluate, procure, deploy, and operationalize Karl as a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) solution.

The purpose of this document is to ensure **clarity, transparency, and consistency** across all stages of the purchasing lifecycle.

## **Step-by-Step Instructions**

### **1\. Open Microsoft Fabric**

*   Log in to your Microsoft Fabric environment.
*   Navigate to the **Workloads** section from the left sidebar.
*   Locate **Karl - Insights (preview)** in the list of available workloads.

![](/images/extracted/img_cc31ca32.png)

### **2\. Go to Azure Marketplace**

*   On the Karl workload page, click the button that redirects you to the **Azure Marketplace**.
*   Scroll to the bottom of the Marketplace listing and click **"Get it on Azure Portal"**.

![](/images/extracted/img_61f8659d.png)

![](/images/extracted/img_08f32eaf.png)

### **3\. Subscribe via Azure Portal**

*   You’ll be redirected to the Azure Portal subscription page.
*   Select your **Azure subscription** and create or choose a **resource group**.
*   Choose the **Free Tier** plan:
*   30-Day Free Trial
*   5,100 Credits included

### **4\. Disable Auto-Renewal**

*   To ensure the subscription ends after the free trial, toggle **Auto-renew** to **Off**.

### **5\. Review and Subscribe**

*   Click **Review + Subscribe**.
*   On the next page, click **"Configure account now"**.
*   You’ll also receive this link via email.
*   Clicking this triggers the subscription activation (may time to take a few minutes).

![](/images/extracted/img_e06109ba.png)

### **6\. Return to Fabric**

*   After activation, you’ll be redirected back to the Karl workload page.
*   You can now go to any of your workspaces and **add Karl as a new item**.
*   The workload will be available under your **tenant's ID**.
