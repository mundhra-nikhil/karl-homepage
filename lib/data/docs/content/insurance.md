# Insurance

## Overview
Insurance carriers operate in a data-rich industry where speed, accuracy, and customer experience directly impact profitability. Manual claims processing, fragmented risk assessment, and reactive customer engagement create bottlenecks that increase costs and reduce competitiveness. AI and modern data platforms enable instant decisions, personalized products, and proactive risk management.

## What We Do
- **Automate claims processing** from First Notice of Loss (FNOL) through settlement using AI that assesses damage, detects fraud, and determines coverage in seconds
- **Enhance underwriting accuracy** with predictive models that leverage alternative data sources for faster, more precise risk assessment
- **Personalize customer engagement** through usage-based products, dynamic pricing, and proactive loss prevention recommendations
- **Optimize operational performance** with real-time dashboards that monitor loss ratios, expense ratios, and portfolio health across lines of business

## Key Outcomes
- Reduce claims processing time by 50% while improving fraud detection accuracy by 40%
- Accelerate underwriting decisions by 60% with AI-assisted risk assessment and automated data collection
- Improve customer retention by 25% through personalized products and proactive communication
