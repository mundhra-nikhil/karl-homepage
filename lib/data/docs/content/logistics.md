# Logistics

## Overview
Logistics providers face constant pressure to reduce costs while improving delivery speed and visibility in an on-demand economy. Fuel volatility, labor shortages, and unpredictable demand patterns create operational complexity that legacy systems can't handle. AI transforms logistics from reactive firefighting to proactive, predictive operations that delight customers and protect margins.

## What We Do
- **Optimize route and load planning** with AI that balances cost, service level agreements, and real-time constraints across complex networks
- **Predict demand and capacity** to position inventory, preposition assets, and scale labor before bottlenecks emerge
- **Enhance shipment visibility** with end-to-end tracking, exception management, and predictive ETAs across multiple carriers and modes
- **Automate warehouse operations** through intelligent slotting, picking optimization, and labor forecasting that reduces handling costs

## Key Outcomes
- Reduce transportation costs by 15% through AI-powered route optimization and load consolidation
- Improve on-time delivery performance by 20% with predictive capacity planning and exception management
- Cut warehouse labor costs by 25% through optimized slotting, picking paths, and staffing schedules
