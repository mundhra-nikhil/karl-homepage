## Overview

This document provides an overview of the **pricing structure and credit-based consumption model** for **Karl – Insight Agent**, the enterprise AI analytics platform developed by **Kanerika Inc**. It serves as a reference for customers, partners, and internal stakeholders to understand subscription tiers, credit allocation, usage policies, and cost optimization mechanisms.

The purpose of this document is to ensure **transparency, predictability, and alignment** in pricing and consumption, enabling organizations to effectively plan, monitor, and manage their investment in Karl while maximizing business value.

## Pricing & Credits

Our platform uses a **flexible credit-based system.** **1 credit = \\$0.01 (1 cent)**

Different AI actions consume different credit amounts:

| **Action Type** | **Credits per Action** |
| --- | --- |
| Extraction | 1 credit |
| Chart Generation | 7 credits |
| Q&A | 3 credits |
| Why Analysis | 45 credits |
| Deep Why Analysis | 60 credits |

## Subscription Tiers

All credits are **monthly** and can be used **at any time** — no daily caps. Users can exhaust their entire monthly allocation in a single day if needed.
