# Manufacturing

## Overview
Manufacturers struggle with unplanned downtime, quality escapes, and rising input costs that erode margins in an already competitive market. Legacy systems with fragmented data make it impossible to predict failures, optimize production, or respond quickly to supply chain disruptions. AI and IoT enable smart manufacturing where every machine, process, and decision is data-driven.

## What We Do
- **Predict equipment failures** before they happen using IoT sensors, machine learning, and historical maintenance data
- **Optimize production schedules** in real-time based on machine availability, material constraints, and demand priorities
- **Improve quality control** with automated visual inspection, statistical process control, and root cause analysis
- **Streamize supply chain coordination** between suppliers, production, and distribution to minimize inventory and maximize service levels

## Key Outcomes
- Reduce unplanned downtime by 40% through predictive maintenance and condition-based monitoring
- Improve overall equipment effectiveness (OEE) by 20% with real-time production optimization
- Cut quality escapes by 50% using AI-powered inspection and automated root cause analysis

## Solutions We Offer
- **Predictive Maintenance** - IoT-enabled condition monitoring with failure prediction and work order automation
- **Production Optimization** - Real-time scheduling, throughput analysis, and bottleneck detection
- **Quality Intelligence** - Automated inspection, defect detection, and statistical process control dashboards
- **Supply Chain Orchestration** - Supplier performance monitoring, inventory optimization, and material requirements planning
- **Energy Management** - Real-time monitoring and optimization of utility consumption across facilities
